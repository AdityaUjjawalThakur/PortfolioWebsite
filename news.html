Project Overview

This website is a dynamic and up-to-date news aggregator built to provide users with a clean and efficient news-reading experience. The platform utilizes a free "News API" to fetch and display the latest headlines and articles on its homepage, complete with relevant thumbnails and brief descriptions.

The core functionality of the website is designed for seamless user interaction: clicking on any news thumbnail or headline redirects the user directly to the original news publisher's website. The platform includes a robust **search feature** that allows users to find specific news articles by making a real-time call to the API using a keyword.

For curated content, the navigation bar offers three predefined quick-search options: "IPL", "Politics", and "Finance". Clicking on any of these options instantly provides users with a tailored feed of news on their topic of interest. The website's design prioritizes clarity and ease of use, making it simple for anyone to stay informed.
