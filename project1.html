Project Overview

BlogVerse is a feature-rich, full-stack blogging platform. It is designed to be both a creative hub for authors and an engaging space for readers. The platform provides a secure and intuitive environment for users to register, log in, and manage all aspects of their content.

Each user is provided with a personal dashboard to create, edit, publish, and delete blog posts with a seamless experience. The platform includes a powerful rich text editor and supports image uploads, giving creators full control over their work.

Beyond creation, authors can access a detailed analytics dashboard to track blog views and reader engagement. Readers can interact with posts by liking and leaving comments. An exclusive admin panel ensures site-wide management and user permissions, maintaining a safe and well-moderated community.
